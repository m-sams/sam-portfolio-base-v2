The Rite of Passage Donut

The "Rite of Passage Donut" is a Blender tutorial project that almost every beginner experiences in order to learn the basics of Blender. My first attempt at the Donut was back in Blender version 2.0, and what's on this page is the same donut implemented in Blender version 3.0.1.
